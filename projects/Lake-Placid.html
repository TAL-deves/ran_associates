Design Philosophy

Presenting Lake Placid – the glittering new landmark on Gulshan’s skyline. Strategically located in the resurgent enclave of Gulshan, every facet here, big or small, is polished to absolute preciousness. Luminous spaces abound - from the vast living rooms, to the huge bedrooms, to the expansive landscaped green areas. With majestic views of Banani Lake - perfectly framed by the massive bay windows - and the thoughtful East-West layout, each 3 bedroom residence at RANGS Lake Placid reflects a magnificent obsession with creating timeless value.
