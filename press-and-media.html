Rangs Properties Limited has participated REHAB Winter Fair

The Real Estate and Housing Association of Bangladesh (REHAB) has organised the five-day fair from 24-28 December, 2019 at Bangabandhu International Conference Center, Sher-e-Bangla Nagar where Rangs Properties has participated this year as a co-sponsor residing in stall no.13 showcasing their newest residential project ‘Rangs Torukabbo’ along with their fleet of other high end luxurious commercial & residential projects bundled with exciting deals and packages.

Rangs Properties Limited has launched a new project called ‘Rangs Torukabbo’ adjacent to Monipur High School located at Shewrapara Mirpur in this year’s REHAB Winter Fair as a co-sponsor residing at stall no. 13. The five day fair will continue from 24-28 December, 2019 at Bangabandhu International Conference Center with amazing offers and customized deals with an assortment of luxurious residential and commercial projects.
