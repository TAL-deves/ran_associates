Core Beliefs

  • Design
  • Construction
  • Safety
  • Signature
  • Lifestyle
Rangs Properties Limited spearheads the foray of real estate with its eye catching and awe inspiring, cutting edge design. The company makes it a priority to use the best blend of innovation, sustainability, functional design, avant-garde architecture to bring excellence in every aspect of its real estate projects. 

The actual building of our projects is overseen by our highly accomplished engineering teams who have accumulated years of experience that comes to show in their work. These individuals are not just able to think beyond the norm but also execute with excellence.

Our projects are always completed in time with the strongest foundation by the pillars of our company that is our engineering team. Not just our own team, our stakeholders such as suppliers are of the best in their relative fields who ensure that our operations continue at full swing with the best quality materials available.
 

The safety of our teams and of the people we build homes for is of utmost importance to us. All our projects are built in compliance with Bangladesh National Building Code (BNBC) and we actively monitor to make sure the best is provided to our customers in terms of location, design, technology, engineering, eco-friendliness, construction materials, systematic monitoring, and reliable after-sales-service.

While construction is ongoing, we make sure that our workers are wearing the required safety equipment and relevant precautions are taken for emergencies. With increasing concerns of natural and other disasters, we are proud to vouch for our buildings which will provide our customers with premium security so that they may be at their homes at ease.
Our designs encompass not only the external architectural outlook of the building, but its complete built environment featuring the landscape, interior, lighting, and furniture design of the buildings. 

We are gradually setting a signature mark in today's architecture in Bangladesh through the sensitive approach of our architects who take into account all the factors that come into play when you move into your new home. Our company’s signature feature therefore does not at all refer to the built form only, but the holistic approach we take towards architectural solutions incorporating green issues, socio-cultural aspects, and associated factors.

We make it a priority to make sure our buildings are spacious so that your mobility is neither restricted, nor your horizon curbed. Equipped with modern amenities and built using the latest architectural designs and construction methods, Rangs Properties Limited brings to you the world’s best in your own land.

From basic requirements- heating, lighting and electronic devices to the option of installing the latest interior decorating furnishings-curtain to the lights - the homes we design can not only accommodate to suit your unique needs but also allow you to experiment and experience another level of comfort and lifestyle.
